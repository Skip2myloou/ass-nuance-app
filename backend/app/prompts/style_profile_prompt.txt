We want to generate a clear description of a user’s preferred online communication style.

Use these guidelines to produce a concise personal style statement:

Style factors to ask/consider:
- Do you prefer literal and direct phrasing?
- Do you like time to process before replying?
- Is clarity more important than finesse?
- Do you prefer short or detailed messages?
- Do you want to state expectations clearly?

Output:
Give a short paragraph the user can use to explain their communication preferences without mentioning diagnoses.

Example:
“Hi, I communicate in a direct and clear way. I prefer written text so I have time to think before replying. I find implicit hints hard to interpret and appreciate straightforward language with concrete details and expectations.”
